# SAT Math Content Hierarchy

## Purpose

This document converts official SAT Math domain descriptions into public-safe item-writing targets for an original diagnostic. It is a design parameter document, not a source of copied items.

## Domains And Subdomains

### Algebra

Evidence target: analyze, fluently solve, and create linear equations and inequalities, plus analyze and solve systems using multiple techniques.

MVP subdomains:

- Linear equations in one variable.
- Linear equations in two variables.
- Linear functions.
- Systems of two linear equations in two variables.
- Linear inequalities in one or two variables.

### Advanced Math

Evidence target: demonstrate readiness for more advanced math through nonlinear expressions, equations, and functions.

MVP subdomains:

- Equivalent expressions.
- Nonlinear equations in one variable.
- Systems of equations in two variables.
- Nonlinear functions.
- Quadratic, exponential, polynomial, rational, radical, and absolute-value relationships.

### Problem-Solving And Data Analysis

Evidence target: apply quantitative reasoning to ratios, rates, proportional relationships, data, probability, and statistical claims.

MVP subdomains:

- Ratios, rates, proportional relationships, and units.
- Percentages.
- One-variable data distributions and measures of center/spread.
- Two-variable data models and scatterplots.
- Probability and conditional probability.
- Inference from sample statistics and margin of error.
- Evaluating statistical claims from studies and experiments.

### Geometry And Trigonometry

Evidence target: solve geometry, measurement, triangle, trigonometry, and circle problems.

MVP subdomains:

- Area and volume formulas.
- Lines, angles, and triangles.
- Right triangles and trigonometry.
- Circles.
